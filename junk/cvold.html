SKILLS AND INTERESTS

Cloud.

VMware, Amazon Web Services (AWS) and OpenStack expert. Worked with
VMware and AWS over a decade. Strong focus on migration from physical to virtual,
cloud architecture and security.

Linux.

Worked with Linux since year zero. Started administering Linux servers
professionally working as a LAMP Programmer for an SME. Since then continually
moved up in scale and complexity to now comfortably working on the most complex
enterprise scale installations.

Security.

Compliance Checking, auditing, penetration testing, patch risk analysis
weighing up both security and stability risks. Carried out security work up to and
including complete organisational security audits. Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH).

Systems.

Skilled in unravelling complex systems, frequently without the aid of
documentation, discovering how they work then making them do more. Skilled in
building servers that run without unplanned downtime for years. Comfortable with
Agile, RUP and Waterfall work-flows.

Migrations.

Windows to Linux, physical to virtual, physical to cloud. Worked on
physical moves, network migrations, consolidating servers on mainframes, upgrades,
resource optimisation. Adept in smooth change control and enjoy regular
stakeholder communication.
